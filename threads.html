THREADS

ABOUT THE APP

Threads is an online platform that allows users to not only browse and shop the latest trends, but provides a space for users all around the world to sell their valued goods! With basic and familiar features, the app is intuitive and minimalistic, providing users the best and easy experience.

THE PROCESS

The design process consist of ideation, thinking of something that not only is aesthetically pleasing, but useful and easy to use. Then moving on to user flows and low-fidelity wireframes, I planned out how the app would function and sketched with pen and paper to have a better visual understanding. With that, I was able to create a mid-fidelity prototype which allowed me to conduct a user testing. With the feedback provided, I created my high-fidelity wireframe and mockups.

USER TESTINGS

JORDAN HERZFELD said: One thing I would advice you to do is to put a rectangle over some of your buttons and just delete the fill and outline - use that as a button over your real buttons so that the user has an easier way to touch them. For example your plus button when selling something is a bit small.

Solution: I made the plus button slightly larger and grouped/combined everything together to be one button for easier use.

FAYAZ ALEEM said: Only thing I don't like is the icons in the navigation bar. It doesn't seem to suit the app's theme.

Solution: I changed the lining size from 2 to 1.5 through all icons for a sleeker, clean look. I also changed out notifications and profile icon to more suited ones for familiarity.

BEBE HASSAIN said: Everything is straightforward and runs smoothly. Only thing is nav. bar is dragged along on shopping portion.

Solution: I redesigned the nav. bar and placed it accordingly and confirmed fixed position when scrolling.
